FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What can Farmlovers do for me?

You already have many buyers, so why join Farmlovers? First of all, you can attract new buyers who pay better and order more. But the most important thing is to talk to them and grow what they need. Wouldn't it be great if you could plan your seeding according to future market demand? When you join Farmlovers, you become part of a strong community that can help you grow and secure your future.

Who is behind Farmloves?

An international group of agricultural experts and professionals in web technologies and marketing. They are present on three continents (North America, Europe and Asia) and their goal is to support all local farmers and their family members and secure their future by bringing their freshest and healthiest products to market.

How much does the membership cost?

The basic package will always be free. Over time, we will offer some additional services for even greater visibility, which will be charged.

Of course! Announce your harvest and invite buyers to be the first to sign up!

Can I use Farmlovers to better plan my production?

Yes! You can avoid surpluses or poor prices by growing for the market. Are sweet potatoes preferred over regular potatoes? Is barley in particular demand? Get valuable insights into what customers in your community prefer and plan your production accordingly.
